About

Hello, my name is Nadine Fiedler, a UI/UX Designer originally from Germany and currently based in Japan. I'm passionate about finding engaging solutions for user problems and creating immersive and visually appealing designs.

After graduating from university with a degree in Japanese Studies, I came to Japan in November 2020 to work for a Japanese company. During this time, I discovered my passion for UI/UX Design, and in November 2023, I graduated in UI Design at Careerfoundry.

My experience of living in different countries gives me a unique perspective and understanding of both the Western and Japanese markets, enabling me to find diverse solutions based on user needs from various regions.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy reading books in different languages, exploring new cafes, or relaxing while playing video games. I'm also a passionate self-learner with an urge to constantly improve my current skill set or acquire a totally new one.
